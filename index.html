About Me

Full Stack Developer | San Francisco, CA

Hi, my name is Cameron. I'm a full stack developer who specializes in the design and development of MERN stack applications. I've worked professionally as a software consultant in the insurance industry where I built React UIs on top of Java/Spring servers. While teaching English in Tokyo, I launched a Japanese language learning platform to help myself and others explore linguistic insights. I'm now based in San Francisco and work remotely as a contractor for a technology school located in Paris.

I'm actively searching for a product-focused full stack engineer position based in the US. I value growth, ownership, autonomy and collaboration in my role as a developer and I enjoy taking on new challenges that empower me to learn while applying my skills. In my next role, I see myself wearing many hats (old and new) while shipping projects with high impact alongside an energized team.

Beyond software development, I've completed freelance projects as a web developer, graphic designer, aerial photographer, and STEM instructor. I love learning and sharing what I know.

When I'm not building something I usually like to play music, explore urban or natural places, and socialize with friends. I'm always open to expanding my network and collaborating. Let's connect!
